HOW TO GET TO THE HOTEL?

 

Direction #1: Once you enter Ravda coming from Burgas or Varna, take left on “Svoboda” Str. which turns into “Izgrev “ Str. You will see TROPICANA Family Hotel on your left just before you reach “Makedonia” Str.


Direction#2: If you miss to turn left on “Svoboda” Str. as described in Direction#1, keep on driving straight on “St. Cyril and Metodius” Str. until you reach the center of Ravda and turn left on “Makedonia” Str. Then take the firs left (“Izgrev” Str.) and you’ll see TROPICANA Family Hotel on your right.


Direction#3: If you enter Ravda coming from Nesebar, turn right on “Izgrev” Str. and you’ll see TROPICANA Family Hotel on your right.

Just 3 km from the Ancient town of Nesebar and 5 km from the Sunny Beach Resort, in the very center of Ravda, across from the Luna Park amusement place......Family Hotel TROPICANA**

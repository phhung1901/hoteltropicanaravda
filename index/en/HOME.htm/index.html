Hotel Tropicana - Ravda

TROPICANA Family Hotel is located in the very center of Ravda Resort, just across from the Luna Park – an Amusement Park for kid’s entertainment. The distance to the Central Beach is only 300 m. There is convinient public transportation between Ravda and the most popular Bulgarian sea resort – Sunny Beach /4 km/ or the ancient town of Nesebar /3 km/. The distance to the nearest airport – Burgas Airport/BOJ/ is 25 km.
TROPICANA Family Hotel was build in 2005 and offers maximum comfort to the guests. They may choose from 13 Double and Twin rooms, 6 Triple /Family/ rooms and a Studio accommodating up to 5 guests. Each room has enough space to fit in a comfortable Extra bed at additional cost. The facilities in the rooms include: Air Conditioning, Hairdryer, Mini Fridge, TV set with Cable, Private Bathroom with Shower, Spacious Terrace with Table, Chairs and Clothes dryer, Safe-deposit Box and Wireless Internet connection.

The interesting Mediterranean style architecture, walls and ceilings made of natural stone, antic furniture and crystal chandeliers in the lobby, unique interior and exterior design and the welcoming service definitely create a relaxing and exotic atmosphere. There is a swimming pool at the tropical garden /1.50 m deep/ and a little kid’s pool /0.50 m deep/.The Pirate Boat at the bottom of the garden is a favourite spot for the children to play. There is also a free of charge Mini Fitness Center for those of our clients who care about their body shape.
Enjoy your morning cup of coffee or your afternoon cocktail drink under the exotic hand-made canopy setting, relaxing or surfing the web, checking your e-mails or skype with your friends and relatives. There are numerous shops, restaurants, pizzarias and taverns around. Convinient free of charge shuttle buses will take you either to Nesebar Aqua Park or Sunny Beach Aqua Park.
TROPICANA Family Hotel is an exellent choice for summer vacation at a very affordable price for families with children or organized parties and for couples as well. TROPICANA is a vacation type hotel and works seasonally from middle of June to middle of September. Many guests return to this popular and very well maintained hotel year after year and bring more and more friends.
TROPICANA Family Hotel is a place where clients become friends!
